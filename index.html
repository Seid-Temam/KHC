About Us

Welcome to Kality Health Center

Kality Health Center is a public health facility serving the residents of Akaki Kality Sub-City with accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services. As part of Addis Ababa's city-wide health system, our center plays a critical role in improving community health outcomes and delivering essential medical services to all, regardless of background or income.

Mission

  • A quality-assured, equitable, and accessible healthcare service
  • Disease prevention
  • Better health and well-being for all.

Vision

  • To see the residents of Woreda 07 healthy and productive
  • Quality healthcare for a healthier community.
  • Healthy community, vibrant future.

values

  • We treat every individual with empathy and respect.
  • We are committed to providing the highest quality of care.
  • We act with honesty and maintain trust in all our services.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services are offered at the Kality health center?

We provide outpatient care, maternal and child health services, vaccinations, family planning, health education and more.

What are the working hours of the health center?

We are open Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM but for emergencies, we are available 24/7.

Do I need an appointment to receive services?

For most services, you can walk in. However, appointments are required for some specialized care.

Is the health center service free?

Basic services are free or low-cost, depending on the type of care and government support.

Can I get vaccinated at the center?

Yes, we offer routine immunizations for children and adults.

What should I bring when visiting the health center?

Please bring your fayda national ID and any medical records or referral papers you have.
